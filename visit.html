Location

Getting Here

A bus departs from both Durrës and Tirana, making it easy to visit the museum from these nearby cities. The buses run regularly, ensuring a convenient and comfortable journey for all visitors.
For detailed bus schedules, please visit Transport Schedule.

The museum offers a spacious parking area that is available to all visitors free of charge. You can enjoy your visit without the worry of finding a parking spot, as there is plenty of space to accommodate everyone. Whether you arrive by car or bus, parking is hassle-free and convenient.
