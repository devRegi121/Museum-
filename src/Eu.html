European Textile Collection Exhibition

Celebrate the artistry and heritage of textiles at the European Textile Collection Exhibition, showcasing timeless designs and craftsmanship. From February 10 to February 25, 2025, explore the intricate patterns and techniques that have adorned European cultures for centuries.

This exhibition presents an extraordinary array of textiles, from medieval tapestries to contemporary couture, highlighting their cultural and historical significance. Interactive displays and expert talks provide insight into the stories woven into every thread.

Whether you’re captivated by fashion or fascinated by history, this event offers a unique opportunity to appreciate the skill and creativity behind Europe’s finest textiles. Dive into a rich tapestry of art and craftsmanship.

Dates: February 10 – February 25, 2025
Daily: 10.00–17.00 (Saturdays 20.30)
Location: Gallery 2
The Margaret Rivers Art Pavilion
Tickets: Adults from £42, Members and under-16s free.
