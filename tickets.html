Admission Tickets

  • 10 € for Adults, 8 € for Seniors, 2 € for Kids
  • 5 € for Students with a valid student ID
  • 2 € for Visitors with a disability; free for a caregiver of a visitor with a disability (in person only)
  • Free for Children under 5 and Members

  • Online tickets must be presented at the museum entrance, either as a printed copy or displayed digitally on your device.
  • Tickets can also be purchased directly at the museum entrance if you prefer to buy them in person.
